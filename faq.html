Dorzet FAQ

1. Profile & Rate

1. How to report/rate your experience?
Ans: Navigate to "My Network" → "Expressed Interest/My Contacts" → "Rate/Report."

2. How to update skills & profile?
Ans: Go to "Profile" → "Settings" → "Edit Profile."

2. Referral Bonus Issues

1. Referral bonus not credited.
Ans: If your referee did not mention the Referral ID at the time of registration, the referral amount will not be credited.

2. Commission not credited.
Ans: If your referee did not mention the Referral ID at the time of registration, the commission will not be credited.
